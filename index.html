About

A VA who'd rather learn the backend than just talk about it.

I started in U.S.-facing customer service — healthcare with Optum, ticketing with Ibex, and general support with Alorica — three years of handling real accounts, real deadlines, and real customers who needed a clear answer, not a script.

To move into e-commerce work, I didn't just take a course — I built two live Shopify stores from nothing: North & Nest, an office and home essentials brand, and Thistle & Bloom, a facial skincare line. Catalog structure, product photography sourcing, collection setup, discount launches, inventory counts — all of it, solo, so I could show up to interviews with a store I actually operate, not just a certificate.

I'm early in my e-commerce career and I say so plainly. What I bring is reliability, an obsessive habit of double-checking data, and the composure that comes from three years of keeping U.S. customers calm on the other end of a queue.
